# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want the Pridge community — issues, pull requests, discussions and any other project space — to be welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, identity or the hardware they're trying to get printing.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in feedback and disagreement.
- Stay focused on the technical problem, not the person.
- Accept differing viewpoints and experience levels gracefully.
- Assume good faith, and give people room to correct mistakes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults or personal attacks, public or private.
- Discriminatory jokes, language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information (addresses, tokens, credentials, etc.) without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues or pull requests.
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all Pridge project spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions) and when representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private security advisory or contacting the maintainer directly through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and investigated, and a response will be tailored to the circumstances — from a warning to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of whoever reports an incident.
